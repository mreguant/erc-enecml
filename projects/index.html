Learn about our projects studying energy markets

Our research is focused on exploring how electricity markets are evolving in their decarbonization process, and to understand the economic implications of the energy transition.

In most of our project, we use detailed high-frequency data to examine the responses of firms and households to this evolving environment, and study alternative policies with counterfactual modeling tools.
