Policy Work

We contribute to the discussion on how to better design electricity markets and cope with the impacts of the energy transition by engaging in policy work for different governments and institutions. Our work focuses on providing evidence-based insight to help in the policy design and implement as well as to contribute to bridge the gap between research and practical implementation.
