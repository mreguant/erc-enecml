Demand Side Behavior

We examine the responses and impact to households from the energy transition. In many of hour projects, we use hourly consumption data (sometimes from smart meters), to understand how and when consumers get their electricity, how they are responding to regulatory changes, how they are impacted by the energy crisis, etc.
